# Code of Conduct

Tunely Home Assistant Add-ons is maintained as a professional and respectful
open source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, constructive, and specific.
- Assume good intent while asking for clarification when needed.
- Keep discussion focused on the technical work and project goals.
- Respect differing levels of experience and background.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing issues, pull requests, or reviews.
- Intentionally submitting harmful code, credentials, or misleading information.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests; or
restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project.

To report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through
GitHub. If no private channel is available, open a minimal public issue asking
for maintainer contact and do not include sensitive details.
